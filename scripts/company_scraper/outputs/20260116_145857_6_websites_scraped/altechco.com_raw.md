## History
Altech was founded in 1984. Houston lacked a computer and electronics recycler at the time.
We recognized the emerging need as an opportunity that we were determined to address.
We became the first computer and electronics recycler in Houston, the fourth largest city in the US.
We continue to innovate in resale, recycling, reverse logistics, refurbishment, tear down, data destruction, testing and processing for computers and electronics.
We move ahead today with the same principles and added R2v3, ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and ISO 45001 certifications.
We are proud to be celebrating our 41st year in 2025.
## Services
  * ITAD Management
  * Resale of excess equipment and inventory
  * Responsible recycling of computers and electronics
  * Audit, testing and fulfillment services for computer and electronic inventory
  * Tear down of computers and electronics
  * In-house shredding and data wiping

Altech se fundó en 1984. En ese momento, Houston carecía de un reciclador de computadoras y productos electrónicos.
Reconocimos la necesidad emergente como una oportunidad que estábamos decididos a abordar.
Nos convertimos en el primer reciclador de computadoras y productos electrónicos en Houston, la cuarta ciudad más grande de Estados Unidos.
Seguimos innovando en reventa, reciclaje, logística inversa, reacondicionamiento, desmontaje, destrucción de datos, pruebas y procesamiento para computadoras y electrónica.
Hoy avanzamos con los mismos principios y agregamos las certificaciones R2v3, ISO 9001, ISO 14001 e ISO 45001.
We are proud to be celebrating our 41st year in 2025.
