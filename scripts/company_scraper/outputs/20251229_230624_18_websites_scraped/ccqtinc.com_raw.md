# How It All Started
At CCQT Inc., we began with a vision to revolutionize the electronics recycling and wholesale industry. Over the years, we've expanded our operations across North America and now serve clients in over 22 countries. Our dedicated team, united by a shared commitment to quality and sustainability, propels us ahead. We take pride in our unity and teamwork, which fuel our continuous growth and success. Our focus remains steadfast on creating a greener future through sustainable practices. Together, we are building a legacy of environmental stewardship and global impact.
## Our Core Values
### Trust & Security
We prioritize data security and maintain the highest industry standards.
### Customer Focus
Our clients' success drives every decision we make.
### Sustainability
Committed to environmental responsibility in all operations.
### Excellence
Striving for perfection in every transaction and interaction.
## Our Team
Our team at CCQT Inc. is driven by expertise and passion for the tech industry. As a family-run business, we bring unparalleled commitment to the wholesale and distribution of consumer technology goods. Our journey began in Canada in early 2021, and by 2023, CCQT Inc. had already exported 50,000 units of used electronics worldwide. We expanded our footprint by opening our first facility in California in 2023. This rapid growth reflects our team's dedication to excellence and innovation in serving our global clientele.

Buyback Program
# Turn Your Old Tech Into Value
Our secure and environmentally responsible buyback program helps you unlock value from outdated technology while earning cash or credit toward future purchases.
## Eligible Devices
Smartphones and tablets
Laptops and desktops
Servers and networking equipment
Components such as hard drives, RAM, GPUs
Other electronic devices, depending on condition and model
## Buyback Process
1
### Initial Inquiry
Clients submit a list or inventory of assets intended for buyback.
2
### Assessment
CCQT evaluates the devices based on grade, model, and market value. If required, photos or physical inspection may be requested.
3
### Offer
A formal buyback quote is provided within 2-3 business days.
4
### Logistics
CCQT coordinates pickup or shipping (labels provided where applicable).
5
### Testing and Validation
Upon receiving the assets, CCQT verifies the condition and functionality.
6
### Payment
Final payment or store credit is issued within 5-7 business days after validation.
## Data Security Assurance
All devices received through our buyback program are handled in strict compliance with data privacy regulations. We provide certified data destruction upon request, and all storage devices are wiped or destroyed using NIST-compliant procedures. Certificates of data destruction are available for corporate and institutional clients.
## Why Choose CCQT's Buyback Program?
Unlock hidden value from unused or aging tech
Simplify your upgrade cycle by offsetting costs
Ensure compliant and secure disposition of sensitive devices
Reduce environmental impact by supporting electronics reuse and recycling
Get fast quotes and prompt payment from a trusted global electronics partner

Asset Grading Standards
# Simple, Transparent Asset Classification
Our standardized A, B, C grading system ensures consistent, fair evaluation of all electronic assets
## Mobile Phones Grading
A
### Excellent Condition
A product in excellent condition with no signs of wear, no cosmetic faults and no scratches on screen. This product has been fully tested and is 100% functional.
B
### Very Good Condition
A product in very good condition. Signs of wear might be visible. Light to moderate scratches on screen. Light to moderate signs of wear on cosmetic features. This product has been tested and is 100% functional.
C
### Good for Refurbishment
This product will show signs of wear. Moderate to heavy scratches on screen. Moderate to heavy markings/etches/dents on its cosmetic features. This product has been tested for basic functions and is ideal for companies looking to refurbish the phones.
## Laptops Grading
A
### Excellent Condition
A product in excellent condition with no signs of wear, no scratches on screen, no cosmetic damage. This product has been fully tested and is 100% functional.
B
### Very Good Condition
A product in very good condition, with light to moderate signs of wear. Light to moderate scratches on casing and cosmetic features. Screen will be in very good shape with little to no signs of use. This product has been fully tested and is 100% functional.
C
### Suitable for Refurbishment
A product that shows moderate to heavy signs of use. Moderate to heavy scratches on housing, markings on cosmetic features. Light to moderate scratches, prints or dots on screens. Could have a chipped corner in housing or lower end plastic panel below the screen. This product has been tested for basic functions only and is very suitable for refurbishment before use or resale.
