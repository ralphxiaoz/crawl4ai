## **Our Service**
**As a R2V3 certified company, C & C Lifestyle Inc is dedicated to repurpose used or retired electronics and materials by bringing them back to life and extracting it’s surplus-value. **
### Custom Tailoled Solutions
Allow**C & C Lifestyle Inc** design a program for your company that eliminates the risk of data breaches while increasing your profits. We have established partnerships with R2V3 certified downstream companies to develop solutions to ensure your recycling processes run more efficiently and safely.
### Driverse Sales Network
The team at **C &C Lifestyle Inc** has built a unique collaborative supply chain, including R2V3 certified partners in different states. This supply chain allows us to provide your company with the most profitable solutions.
### Asset Recovery
Excess inventory is a key component of our company. Partnering with sellers in other countries, we are able to create the highest value for your company’s excess product, and we emphasize consistency and accuracy in all steps of our process.
### Reverse Logistics
**C & C Lifestyle Inc**‘s downstream supply chain is very complete. Devices can be safely and effectively guaranteed, while ensuring our customer to generate maximum revenue. Our streamlined processes ensure less equipment errors, and we monitor the final step until the waste enters the furnace.
### Data Sanitization
Maintaining a variety of proven processes and the most advanced technology available, **C & C Lifestyle Inc** ensures all existing data is sanitized from devices in the most secure way. Records are kept in each step of the erasure process and we only use industry accredited sanitization and verification solutions.
### Recycling
**  
C & C Lifestyle Inc** will rationally extract maximum value while avoiding environmental impact and minimizing waste. By cooperating with downstream recyclers that comply with R2V3, the process of refurbishment, repair and recycling of equipment is within control.

### ****Who We Are****
Members in C & C Lifestyle Inc has been in the business since the relase of iPhone 3. We evolved through many digital transitions, continuing to stay one step ahead. Our team has witnessed numerous changes in the industry throughout the years and has become an integral part, while ensuring limited environmental impact. Our leadership team brings decades of experience in the wireless business and we’re proud to say that we’ve renewed and removed data from millions of devices. Throughout our combined years in business, we’ve stood the test of time and have fostered relationships in all aspects of the wireless business. Today, we’re grateful to have industry partners that trust and rely on us. Thanks to a uniquely diverse team, we are able to constantly educate our team on the most environmentally efficient practices with the utmost value placed on their health and safety.
The impacts of electronic waste on the environment transcend communities throughout the world. That is why we have a responsibility to safely repurpose products that would otherwise be discarded. Through our relationships with our partners, we have the ability to reclaim and repurpose most wireless devices. Devices that are unusable are stripped of their components and the individual pieces can be reused or recycled. We believe every device we divert from landfills makes an impact and reinforces our core beliefs to our team, partners and customers. Each repurposed device in the hands of a new owner, enriches global connectivity while contributing to sustainable change.
Our legacy is important to us. Sustainability, experience, quality, and adaptability strengthen that legacy and drive our business forward.
### **What We Do**
We work with different manufacturers, companies, and schools to help handle their unwanted equipments: including computers, mobile phones, accessories and other office equipments. Customer data security is extreamly important. We have been strictly abiding by and implementing the R2 V3 procedures to clear data from all devices, protect all sensitive information for customers, repaire/renew hardwares as many as possible, and use the most environmentally friendly and scientific methods. Our downsteam will recycle materials from non working equipments to avoid contamination and waste. Our company also attaches great importance to the safety of team employees. They’re all precious assets of the company. We grow and work together for the health of the earth.
