## Experts in the Recycling of Electronics
About us 
Welcome to E-Waste Gurus, your trusted partner in sustainable electronics recycling and refurbishment. With a mission to combat the growing challenge of electronic waste in California and beyond, we are dedicated to providing responsible and environmentally-friendly solutions for the disposal and repurposing of outdated electronics.
At E-Waste Gurus, we understand the critical importance of properly managing electronic waste to protect our planet and its resources. That’s why we operate as [licensed participants](https://www2.calrecycle.ca.gov/Electronics/CEW/ParticipantsDirectory/Details/1642) in California’s Covered Electronic Waste Recovery program, ensuring that every electronic device brought to us for recycling is handled with care and responsibility. Additionally, we proudly hold R2V3 Certification, a globally recognized standard that reflects our commitment to upholding rigorous environmental, health, and safety practices throughout our operations.
Our approach to electronics recycling extends beyond mere regulatory compliance. We aim to create a positive impact on both our local communities and the environment by refurbishing electronics whenever feasible. Through our specialized recycling program, we breathe new life into outdated devices, either by donating them to non-profits and local schools or by offering them for sale at affordable prices to bolster small businesses in California.
At E-Waste Gurus, we are more than just a recycling company — we are stewards of sustainability, exponents for environmental responsibility, and partners in building a cleaner, greener future. Join us in our mission to reduce electronic waste, conserve resources, and give every device a second chance. Reach out to us today to begin your electronics recycling project and be a part of building a cleaner, more sustainable California.

## Your Privacy is Our Priority
Data Destruction 
E-Waste Gurus, is your trusted partner in secure and responsible data destruction services. We adhere to the highest industry standards, ensuring the protection of your sensitive information and the environment. At E-Waste Gurus, we specialize in comprehensive Data Destruction services, offering both off-site Hard Drive Shredding and Wiping/Sanitization using NIST 800-88 methods, with the option to implement DOD methods or any other customized approach to meet your specific needs.
### Our Services:  

  * **Hard Drive Shredding:**  
Secure and reliable, our Hard Drive Shredding service is performed at our dedicated warehouse. Our state-of-the-art shredder is capable of reducing mechanical drives to 1-½” particles and ⅜” for SSD and laptop drives. The fee for hard drive shredding is based on quantity, providing a cost-effective solution for the disposal of your electronic data storage devices.


  * **Wiping/Sanitization:**  
Our wiping service strictly adheres to the comprehensive NIST 800-88 guidelines for the thorough sanitization of data-bearing technology assets. We use HIPAA compliant Industrial Software to get the job done. This complimentary service is extended to drives with resale value or those suitable for reuse in our refurbished systems. To be eligible, drives must be in good health, with Solid State Drives requiring a capacity larger than 500GB, Mechanical SATA drives of 4TB or higher, and SAS Server Drives eligibility is model-specific.
  * **Witnessed Shredding:**  
We value transparency, and you are more than welcome to schedule an appointment to witness the shredding of your drives. Our secure facility with on-site surveillance ensures the process is conducted with the utmost security and professionalism.
  * **Certificate of Destruction:**  
For your peace of mind, we provide a Certificate of Destruction with detailed serial number recording. This certificate serves as tangible evidence that your data has been securely and irreversibly destroyed, meeting regulatory compliance requirements.


### Security Measures:
  * Our facility emphasizes security through on-site surveillance, fostering a secure environment for data destruction activities. With over 60 days of stored footage, we support incident investigations, regulatory compliance, audit trail creation, legal requirements, and ongoing improvement efforts.
  * All drives and data-sensitive electronics, including tablets and phones, along with the shredder, are housed in a specifically designated locked cage during the destruction process, adding an additional layer of physical security.
  * We prioritize confidentiality and maintain the highest level of security throughout the entire process.


Contact us today to safeguard your data with E-Waste Gurus. Our commitment to secure, environmentally friendly data destruction ensures peace of mind for your business. Trust in our expertise for a reliable solution tailored to your needs.

## What We Accept
E-Waste Gurus 
E-Waste Gurus provides compliant recycling services to a variety of markets, including government agencies, large businesses, and private sectors. We collect end-of-life electronics (also known as e-waste) and in partnership with responsible and certified recyclers offer an easy recycling solution for your excess, obsolete, or unused technology equipment.
**Devices that can be dropped off FREE of charge:**
  *     * CRT Monitors and Televisions
    * LCD Monitors and Televisions
    * Plasma Televisions
    * Computers (Laptops and Desktops)
    * Servers & Networking Equipment
    * Cellular Phones & Tablets
    * Telephones, Telecom & Radio equipment
    * Computer Components: Hard Drives, Motherboards, Power Supplies, CPUs, Memory Modules, and more.
    * Lead-Acid batteries (from UPS, cars, and motorcycles), laptop batteries, Li-ION & NI-MH batteries, and Tesla batteries.
    * Power cords, Cables, and power adapters of any kind (Except Coaxial and fiber cables).


**Disposal Fees _will be_ applied:**
  *     * Small fridges, Microwaves – $20 per item
    * Projection Televisions – $20 per TV
    * Desktop Size Printers, Scanners, Fax Machines – $5 per item
    * Mid-Size Printers (height, width or depth is bigger than 18”) – $15 per printer
    * Large Floor-standing Copiers and printers – $65 Per item
    * Industrial Print Centers (Much Larger than regular office copier)- minimum $200 per unit
    * Alkaline Household batteries (AA, AAA, C, D, 9V and similar) – determined by amount
    * Swollen (DDR) Li-ION & NI-MH batteries  – determined by amount.


**Disposal Fees _might_ apply based on the volume of the load or other factors:**
  *     * Toner and Ink Cartridges. Note, for some new, genuine toner cartridges, there may be no charge, and we might even offer a reimbursement. Contact us with the specific models for more details.
    * Miscellaneous Consumer Electronics _if in large qty_ (ie: CD Players, VCRs, toys, remotes)
    * Security Devices (ie: cameras, alarm systems, and similar) 
    * Large Electronics and equipment (ie: Medical Equipment, Empty server Racks)- send pictures for quote


**Items that are not accepted:**
  *     * Appliances (ie: regular size Refrigerators, Washing Machines, dishwashers) – can be dropped off at your local appliance recycling place, or **[Transfer Stations](https://wmr.saccounty.net/Pages/Local-Disposal-and-Recycling-Facilities-.aspx)**
    * Light Bulbs (You can drop them off at Local Home Depot, Batteries Plus Bulbs, or **[Hazardous Waste Stations near you](https://wmr.saccounty.net/Pages/HHW-Dropoff-Centers.aspx)****.** Contact them for more info).
    * Electric items, such as receptacles, motors, light fixtures, kitchen appliances, vacuums, and similar items, that have no hazardous electronic circuit boards in them. The best place to take it to is any metal (non-ferrous) recycling place. You might even get money for that.
    * Metal, Plastic, and other non-electronic items.


Please note, businesses MAY qualify for free pick up, depending on load size, type, and location of the load (please call to inquire).
