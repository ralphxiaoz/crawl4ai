# Our Mission
At Digital Green Global Inc, we believe in providing quality IT Asset Disposition and Electronic Waste Recycling services to our customers with security and integrity. Our business is founded on our core beliefs in reducing environmental impact, providing progressive sustainable solutions, and offering the best value in all facets of our business.

## Digital Green Global IT Asset Management System Services:
![Recycling](https://www.dgglobal.net/images/dg-main_offer-01.jpg)
### Recycling
DG Global has a zero landfill policy and follows all federal and state guidelines when processing e-waste. Our Web-based audit system provides our customers all the critical data they require on their end of life assets.
![Data Security](https://www.dgglobal.net/images/dg-main_offer-02.jpg)
### Data Security
DG Global provides complete data erasure and destruction on multiple storage devices. We utilize a secure erasure process which supports all methods used and recommended.   
  

![Remarketing or Resale](https://www.dgglobal.net/images/dg-main_offer-03.jpg)
### Remarketing/Resale
We provide a wide range of options for excess IT equipment or EOL product that includes resale with a revenue share program or an outright purchase. Our goal is to maximize your return on your excess assets through extensive sales channels.
![Purchasing](https://www.dgglobal.net/images/dg-main_offer-empty.jpg)
![Facility Closures](https://www.dgglobal.net/images/dg-main_offer-05.jpg)
### Facility Closures
DG Global has worked with numerous corporations on facility closures. We provide detailed reporting on all the excess IT assets along with an overall project summary report. 
![Purchasing](https://www.dgglobal.net/images/dg-main_offer-empty.jpg)
![Harvesting](https://www.dgglobal.net/images/dg-main_offer-04.jpg)
### Harvesting
High value components on excess equipment can be utilized for next generation product or existing equipment. This provides a significant reduction in overall systems costs. 
![Reverse Logistics](https://www.dgglobal.net/images/dg-main_offer-07.jpg)
### Reverse Logistics
DG Global provides on-site services along with a detailed audit trail while transporting enterprise IT equipment. Our web-based solution provides detailed reporting by site location. 
![Test and Refurbishment](https://www.dgglobal.net/images/dg-main_offer-06.jpg)
### Test and Refurbishment
The DG Global lab and team of technicians have experience in all types excess IT equipment. We do extensive testing and cleaning for equipment that still has a market value.
