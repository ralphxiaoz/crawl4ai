## FAQ's 
How can companies or individuals request Green Star services?
Companies or individuals interested in our services can contact us through our website, email, or phone. Our team is ready to provide a tailored solution that meets your e-waste recycling and data destruction needs.
What is e-waste and why is it important to recycle it?
E-waste, or electronic waste, encompasses end-of-life electronic devices i.e., servers, computers, smartphones, and LCD Monitors. Recycling e-waste is crucial because it contains harmful substances that can damage the environment if not disposed of properly. Additionally, recycling conserves natural resources, as many components can be reclaimed, reused, or repurposed.
How does Green Star ensure the highest level of data security?
We employ rigorous data destruction methods, physical destruction, and secure logistics and data erasure, compliant with the highest industry standards. Our processes are designed to guarantee that all data is irrecoverably destroyed, providing our clients with peace of mind.
Can Green Star Services Group help with IT asset disposition (ITAD)?
Yes, our Advanced ITAD Services are designed to manage the end-of-life of your IT assets securely, sustainably, and maximize value recovery. We offer a range of services, including logistics, data erasure, refurbishment, and recycling, all customized to align with your asset disposition needs and ESG protocols. 
How does Green Star Services Group contribute to Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) goals?
Green Star integrates with clients’ ESG protocols resulting in reduced environmental impact, ensuring ethical practices, and promoting good governance. Our services support your ESG goals by providing sustainable e-waste recycling solutions, secure data destruction, and tailored IT asset disposition services.
What types of electronic devices does Green Star Services Group recycle?
Green Star recycles a wide range of electronic devices, essentially any device that plugs in or runs on battery, including but not limited to servers, switches, computers, laptops, smartphones, tablets, LCD Monitors. Our goal is to provide a comprehensive recycling solution for all your e-waste and ITAD needs.

## Our Process
Green Star team will process your request and promptly schedule a pickup. 
Materials are securely transported to Green Star facility. 
Once received, materials go through a smart sorting process and a comprehensive report is generated. 
Materials value is assessed and slated for destruction, recycling or remarketing. 
Assets are sorted and categorized as data bearing or non data bearing and pushed to appropriate streams to be processed and sent to the appropriate certified downstream processors. 
Green Star provides Comprehensive reports, of Recycling, of Destruction and any other documentation per clients specific requirements.
