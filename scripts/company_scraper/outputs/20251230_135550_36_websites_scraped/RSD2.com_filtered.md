###### Who we are
RSD2 offers specialized IT asset disposition (ITAD) services, focusing on data sanitization, hard drive destruction, disposal, and shredding, all certified with data destruction certificates. We aim to provide top-tier ITAD services, prioritizing sustainability, data security, and compliance.
With processing facilities and international offices across the globe, RSD2 is trusted by large organizations worldwide for secure data disposal. Our data destruction equipment is capable of handling highly sensitive data.
###### R2V3 Certified
RSD2 is now an R2V3 Certified Recycler, demonstrating their commitment to sustainability, data security, and environmental responsibility.
This certification ensures that their processes meet the highest standards for responsible recycling and reuse of hardware. It reflects their dedication to protecting the environment while delivering exceptional service to their clients.
